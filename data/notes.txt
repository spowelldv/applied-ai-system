This project is a small class demo.

The system uses a short list of songs in data/songs.csv and a few user preferences.

The rules are simple on purpose.

- Genre match matters a lot.
- Mood match matters too.
- Energy is a number from 0 to 1 and we reward closeness to the target.
- There is an acoustic preference.

Limitations:

- The catalog is small.
- Genres are exact text matches, so pop and indie pop do not merge.
- This does not understand lyrics, culture, or real user behavior.
